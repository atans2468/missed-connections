Start By Updating Your Profile Information


It's important that you keep your profile info up to date so that new connections can learn more about you!

Get Posting!


Simply click on the 'Write A Post' box in the thread to start your first post!


All posts are made anonymously so you can feel safe as you post on the Missed Connections thread and reach out to people that you otherwise wouldn't have the chance to meet again!

Understanding The Thread


Here at Missed Connections we worked hard to provide you with an easy to understand, fun, unique thread that will display all posts within a 50 mile radius of your current location.

Accepting Posts


Accepting posts will allow the original author of the post to see some of your profile information. This helps them decide if you are actually the person that they posted about.


This allows the original poster to find potential connections, but keeps their information private until they think that they have found their missing connection

Messaging


After accepting a post, message the other user to get to know them more! The more messages you send, the more profile information will be unlocked!
